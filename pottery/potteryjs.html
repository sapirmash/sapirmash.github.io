Pottery.js
Info
Pottery is one of the oldest human inventions, with first appearances more than 20,000 years ago. This project makes the process of desgining such objects accessible in a digital medium, using Three.js as a platform for 3D computer graphics. You can modify an existing design or create one from scratch and export your designs to an STL file for 3D printing.


Made by Sapir Mashiach.
